Our #1 priority is your pleasure and satisfaction.

Johnstown Peking Chinese Restaurant specialize in superb cuisine in the Cantonese, Hunan, and Szechuan styles. Our casual Asian setting is graceful and warm. The menu features a variety of delicacies prepared with beef, seafood, poultry and pork. Our vegetable dishes are perfect for the health conscious and our soups are fresh prepared and delicious, especially our house special soups. You may dine in or carry out selections from our extensive menu that includes well over 150 items. If you don't see something or have special requests, just ask us and we will do all we can to accommodate you.
